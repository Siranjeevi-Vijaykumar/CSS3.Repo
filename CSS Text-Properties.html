Android


Android is a mobile operating system based on a modified version of the Linux kernel
and other open-source software, designed primarily for touchscreen mobile devices such
as smartphones and tablets. Android is developed by a consortium of developers known as the
Open Handset Alliance, though its most widely used version is primarily developed by Google. It
was unveiled in November 2007, with the first commercial Android device, the HTC Dream, being
launched in September 2008
